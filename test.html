Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking a service with us.

Our average response time is under 2 hours for most areas in Bangalore. In peak seasons (April–June), it may take up to 3 hours. You'll receive a technician confirmation with live tracking via WhatsApp after booking.
All our repairs come with a 90-day service warranty. If the same issue recurs within this period, we will fix it at no additional cost. Spare parts replaced carry the manufacturer's warranty on top of our service warranty.
Yes, absolutely. We source only OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) approved spare parts. We never use duplicate or low-quality parts, ensuring your appliance performs reliably after every repair.
Our pricing is fully transparent. There's a nominal visit/inspection charge (₹199–₹299) which is waived if you proceed with the repair. Before any repair begins, our technician provides a detailed cost breakdown. You only pay for what you approve.
Yes! Our technicians are trained on all major brands available in India — including Daikin, LG, Samsung, Voltas, Blue Star, Carrier, Hitachi, O General, Mitsubishi, Panasonic, and more. Both older and latest models are covered.
An AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) is a yearly service plan that covers scheduled maintenance visits, priority support, and discounts on repairs and parts. It is highly cost-effective — customers typically save 40–60% compared to pay-per-service pricing, and it also extends the life of your appliances significantly.
Yes! ArcticCool operates 7 days a week including Sundays and public holidays. Premium AMC plan customers also get emergency support on weekends. Standard weekend slots fill up fast, so early booking is recommended.
We recommend servicing your AC at least twice a year — ideally before and after summer (March and October). For heavily used ACs or those in dusty environments, quarterly servicing is advisable. Regular servicing improves efficiency, reduces electricity bills, and extends compressor life.
Absolutely! We specialize in commercial HVAC systems including VRV/VRF multi-split systems, cassette ACs, and central cooling setups for offices, hotels, hospitals, and malls. We offer customized AMC plans for businesses with dedicated account managers.
We accept all payment modes — cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, debit/credit cards, and online bank transfers. Payment is made only after the work is completed to your satisfaction. You'll receive a digital invoice via email/WhatsApp.
