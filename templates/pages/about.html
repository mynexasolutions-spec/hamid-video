Our Story

Since 1990, Hamid Video Films has been the premier choice for wedding cinematography and photography in the region. What started as a passion project has grown into a legacy of capturing love stories with artistic vision and technical excellence.

With more than 3,500 weddings captured and 5,000+ happy couples, we bring unparalleled expertise and a personalized touch to every celebration. Our journey has been guided by a simple philosophy: every wedding is unique, and every love story deserves to be told beautifully.

Over three decades, we've witnessed traditions evolve while timeless emotions remain. From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, we've been honored to preserve moments that families cherish for generations.
